Welcome to RaiseMeUp!

Pong, the "round man", is trapped in an underground mine while the water is flooding in. He needs your help to get out!

Objective:
Place cubes onto the grid to build pathways that lead Pong to escape from the rising water.

Controls:
To place a cube: double tap (on mobile device) or click (on PC) on the desires grid
To rotate views: swipe left or right (on mobile devices) or click and drag (on PC)
To toggle AI for the Pong: "=" on keyboard (PC)
To control Pong yourself: "W", "A", "S", "D" on keyboard

*Note: Cubes are limited resources! Make sure you lead the Pong to the coins to earn more cubes.
You can either play single player mode or play with others. You just need to share a room number.
